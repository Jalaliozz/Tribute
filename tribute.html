Tribute to Lorem ipsum

What is Lorem ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is temporary text used to demonstrate the visual form of a document without relying on meaningful content. It is often used to replace text before the final copy of a document is available. This allows designers to consider the form of a webpage, without the meaning of the text heavily influencing the design. This process is called greeking.


Where did Lorem ipsum come from?

Lorem ipsum is a corrupted version of the 'De finibus bonorum et malorum', a 1st century BC text by the Roman statesman and philosopher Marcus Tullius Cicero. It's had words altered, added, and removed to make it nonsensical and improper Latin.


Discovery of Lorem ipsum

Richard McClintok, a latin scholar, can be credited with the discovery of Lorem ipsum. Richard connected Lorem ipsum to Cicero's writing while searching for instances of the Latin word consectetur.


History of Lorem ipsum

1960s

Early versions of Lorep ipsum text have been used in typsetting since at least the 1960s. It was first popularized by advertisements for Letraset, a company that manufactured sheets of typeface.

1980s

Lorem ipsum was introduced to the digital world in the mid 1980s. Aldus corporation employed it in graphic and word-processing templates for its desktop publishing program, Pagemaker.

1980s

Richard McClintock somehow connected Lorem ipsum to Marcus Tullius Cicero

1994

Richard McClintock published his discovery in a letter to editor Before & After magazine, contesting the editor's earlier claim that Lorem ipsum held no meaning.
